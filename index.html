Founded in April 1995, The Khaitan School is a leading CBSE school in Noida. Sprawled over 6.35 acres, its vibrant ambience, sporting facilities, progressive infrastructure and adoption of experiential learning, provide a stimulating environment for the all-round growth and development of children.
